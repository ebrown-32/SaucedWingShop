HOW IT WORKS

RUN THE WING SHOP

1
Take the orders. Hungry folks line up at the counter. Tap one to grab their ticket. Run a few at once; the tickets stack on the left.
2
Fry a batch. Load a basket, drop it in the oil, and yank it up while the meter sits in the green. Cook big to feed several tickets at once.
3
Drench 'em. Send a basket to the bowl, tap a pot to pour, then toss (tap the bowl, hit Space, or flick up) until they're fully coated.
4
Plate it right. Pick a ticket and "Send to plate." Only the wings that ticket needs come over, so the rest of the batch waits in the bowl. Add the sides, dip, and garnish they asked for.
5
Send it out. Hit SERVE. Nail it fast and clean for five stars, fat tips, and streak bonuses. Sink the cash into upgrades.
